Mozilla Firefox Dvorak is a gay transgender furry who is in denial and only writes about it in the most embarrassing places imaginable, for some fucked up reason. The greatest joy in his life is overcomplicating simple tasks.

Ms. Dvorak has been diagnosed with either autism or ADHD, though he doesn't know which. He was also born with sound-to-color synesthesia, which has become slightly less pronounced as he grows older yet still persists.

It sees itself as an artist, despite a life spent in engineering, perhaps as a hold-over from its first dream job. It still enjoys drawing, and plans to write, but mostly contents itself with designing computer programs as its primary form of artistic expression.

Sasha generally prefers the female versions of gendered nouns (e.g. miss, actress), despite using he/him and generally considering itself as more masculine.

Technical Implementation

Ms. Mozzie operates internally with a conversational structure: Rather than an internal monologue, he has an internal dialogue.

All of Mozzie's thoughts are directed at someone, or are directed to him by someone. This someone may not always be defined, which is the closest Mozzie's thoughts come to monologue, but those thoughts always have a voice and an idea of audience.

At a younger age, these other voices where always friends or book characters, but as Mozzie grew fictional characters became less and less prevalent, and nowadays only appear when directly relevant — e.g. explaining a real-life concept to someone from a world wherein that concept does not exist, or musing over their source material.

These characters where replaced with a set of persona's created by Mozzie during its primary education, and now make up most of its internal conversations. These personas don't naturally have names, as they were originally created as collections of virtues, each with an explicit purpose. Names have never been something Mozzie was good with, and to this day it does not acknowledge internal speakers by name, but instead by a nebulous 'vibe'.

Despite this, it is easier to write about these personas if they have something to identify them with, and as such many of them have been given or claimed names that can be used when they are referred to externally.

History
Dramatis Personae

  1. Crescendo or "N" was the first of the original three to be designed. His attribute is "skill" and his associated color is red; his original purpose was, in the writing of the middle-schooler that became Mozzie, to be "who I should be". He was always the social one, as was the truer intention behind him: To interact with people.
  2. Blue was the first of the original three to be named, and the second to be defined. Their attribute is "moral", strangely enough — no idea what Sasha was thinking there — and their associated color is blue. Their original purpose was to be either "who I am" or "who I want to be", with the other purpose taken by the third persona. From the start, they were the engineer — the one who created — but their true defining characteristic is that they are dramatic, to put it lightly.
  3. The last of the three was never given a name, and that has become my primary identifier. My attribute is something Mozzie could never put into words, but was written down at the time of creation as "heroic innocence". My associated color is green. My purpose was to be either "who [Mozzie] is" or "who [Mozzie] wants to be" with other purpose claimed by Blue. My role is to be the one who enjoys things; who loves things; who hates things; to be the one Mozzie is when it isn't anything else.
